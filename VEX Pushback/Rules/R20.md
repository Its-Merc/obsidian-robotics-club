Allowed non-VEX components:
- zip-ties that are of the same size as the ones VEX includes in their products
- hot glue
	- when locking cables in place
- anti-static compound
	- use with extreme moderation
	- dont leave residue on other [[Robot|Robots]], [[Block|Blocks]], or [[Field Element|Field Elements]]
- non-aerosol grease/lubrication
	- use with extreme moderation
		- on surfaces/locations that should not contact [[Field Perimeter]], [[Field]], [[Floor]], [[Block|Blocks]], or other [[Robot|Robots]]
	- prohibited on [[V5 Smart Motor|V5 Smart Motors]] or Smart Motor cartridges
- material being used only for filtering/marking color for a non-rule-breaking sensor 
	- e.g. [[VEX Light Sensor]], [[VEX V5 Vision Sensor]]
- rope/string
	- no length limit
	- can't be thicker than 0.25 inches
