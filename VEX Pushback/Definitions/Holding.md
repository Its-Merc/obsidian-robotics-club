- Trapping
	- limiting movement of opposing [[Robot]] to small or confined area of the [[Field]]
	- one foam tile or less
	- no avenue of escape
	- if the opposing [[Robot]] is not trying to escape, it is not Trapped
- Pinning
	- preventing movement of opposing [[Robot]]
		- using contact with [[Field Perimeter]], other [[Robot]], or [[Field Element]]
- Lifting
	- lifting/tilting a [[Robot]] off of the [[Floor]]
	- preventing a [[Robot]] that's off the floor from returning to the floor can be considered Lifting
